Healthcare Rates Analysis

The Healthcare project was another final project for my Predictive Modeling class, where I utilized R, Excel, and Tableau to analyze Healthcare outcomes and rates based on the results.

Log. Transforms were utilized to fit the data to a Normal Distribution, since the context of the given healthcare data fits into this format well without overfitting/confounding the data.

The Test & Training models along with the Logistic Regression aim to identify the related outcomes and rates according to the available data in the context of Healthcare Insurance Analysis.
